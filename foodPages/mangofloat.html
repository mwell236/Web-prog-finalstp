Mango Float

★★★★★ (1.7k)
DESSERT Icebox Dessert
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 3.33 cups heavy cream, very cold
  • 1.67 can (14 oz) condensed milk, chilled
  • 10 Manila mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 30 pieces graham crackers, (about 3 inches in length) and more for garnish
  • 2 packs graham crackers from 1 box

Preparation

  • Step 1 In a bowl, combine heavy cream and condensed milk. Using a hand mixer on low speed, whip mixture until it reaches firm peaks.
  • Step 2 Gently fold in half of the diced mangoes, making sure not to deflate the whipped cream.
  • Step 3 Arrange one layer of graham crackers on bottom of prepared dish. Spoon ⅓ of cream mixture on crackers and spread to cover.
  • Step 4 Arrange another layer of graham over the cream. Spoon and spread ⅓ of the cream mixture. Repeat with another layer of graham crackers. Spoon and spread remaining ⅓ of the cream mixture on top of crackers.
  • Step 5 Arrange the remaining diced mangoes on top of the cream and garnish with crushed graham crackers.
  • Step 6 Cover tightly with plastic film and freeze for about 4 to 5 hours until firm.
  • Step 7 To serve, lift the cake from the pan using parchment paper overhang as support and transfer onto a serving platter. Cut into serving portions.
