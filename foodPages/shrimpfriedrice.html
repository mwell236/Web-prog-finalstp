Shrimp Fried Rice

★★★★★ (1.1k)
SEAFOOD Rice Meal
Recipe: vanjo merano
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 10 cups cooked white rice
  • 1.88 cup frozen green peas
  • 1.88 cup frozen small shrimp with no shell
  • 1.25 cup carrot diced
  • 1.25 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.63 teaspoon salt
  • 0.63 teaspoon onion powder
  • 7.5 tablespoons butter

Preparation

  • Step 1 Melt the butter in a pan.
  • Step 2 Once the butter starts to get hot (make sure not to get the butter very hot), put-in green peas and carrots. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Step 3 Put the shrimp in the wok and cook for 1 minute.
  • Step 4 Add the rice mixture. Cook for 10 minutes in medium heat while tossing once in a while. Make sure to toss the ingredients while cooking to blend them properly.
  • Step 5 Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve for breakfast or lunch with your favorite dish.
  • Step 6 Share and enjoy!
