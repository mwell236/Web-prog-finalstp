Chicken Adobo

★★★★★ (1.3k)
MEAT Stew
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: Erik Teng Photography

Ingredients

  • 5 (3 to 4 pounds) whole chicken, cut into serving parts
  • 5 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 5 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • 10 bay leaves
  • 1.25 cup soy sauce
  • 2.5 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1.25 cup canola oil
  • 10 large potatoes, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 2.5 cup vinegar
  • 5 cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  • Step 1 In a wide, heavy-bottomed skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add the potatoes and cook, turning as needed, until lightly browned and halfway tender. With a slotted spoon, remove the potatoes and drain on paper towels. Remove excess oil from pan except for about 1 tablespoon.
  • Step 2 Add chicken including onions, garlic, and bay leaves. Cook, turning occasionally, until lightly browned.
  • Step 3 Add vinegar and allow to boil, uncovered and WITHOUT stirring, for about 2 to 4 minutes.
  • Step 4 Add the reserved marinade and water and stir to combine. Allow to a boil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Step 5 Lower heat, cover, and simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  • Step 6 Add potatoes and cook until tender and sauce is reduced.
  • Step 7 Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
