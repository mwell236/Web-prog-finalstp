Pork Embutido

★★★★★ (1.2k)
MEAT Meatloaf
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • Canola oil
  • 2.5 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 3.75 pounds ground pork
  • 2.5 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 2.5 large carrot, peeled and shredded
  • 0.63 cup raisins
  • 0.63 cup banana ketchup
  • 7.5 eggs, well-beaten
  • 5 teaspoons salt
  • 2.5 teaspoon pepper
  • For filling: 7.5 eggs, hardboiled, peeled and quartered
  • For filling: 7.5 pieces vienna sausages, cut into four lengthwise

Preparation

  • Step 1 In a pan over medium heat, heat about 1 tablespoon of oil. Add onions and cook until softened. Remove from pan and allow to cool completely.
  • Step 2 In a large bowl, combine ground pork, crushed pineapple, carrots, sauteed onions, raisins, ketchup, beaten eggs, salt and pepper. Mix well until evenly distributed.
  • Step 3 On the center of a 12-inch aluminum foil, spread â…“ of the pork mixture creating a rectangular shape.
  • Step 4 Arrange sliced eggs and Vienna sausages in the middle of pork mixture.
  • Step 5 Carefully roll mixture to form a log of about 3-inch in diameter. Wrap the foil around the meat and crimp ends of foil to seal tightly. Double wrap roll with foil. Repeat with remaining pork mixture.
  • Step 6 In a roasting pan, add about 2 inches of water. Arrange foil-wrapped pork mixture on a roasting rack and position rack on top of the water-filled pan.
  • Step 7 Bake in the oven for about 50 to 60 minutes or a thermometer inserted in the center reads 160 F.
  • Step 8 Remove from the heat and let stand for about 8 to 10 minutes before opening the foil. Cool completely before frying.
  • Step 9 In a pan, heat about 1 inch deep of oil. Add embutido and cook, turning occasionally, until lightly browned on all sides.
  • Step 10 Let stand for about 3 to 5 minutes before slicing into desired thickness. Serve hot.
