Cassava Cake

★★★★☆ (260)
DESSERT Cake
Recipe: Vanjo Merano
Image: Gustavo Mellossa

Ingredients

  • 3.33 packs grated cassava about 2 lbs total weight
  • 3.33 cups coconut milk
  • 0.83 12 oz. can evaporated milk
  • 5 pieces egg
  • 0.42 cup butter melted
  • 10 tablespoons cheddar cheese grated
  • 0.83 cup condensed milk
  • 23.33 tablespoons granulated white sugar
  • 3.33 tablespoons flour
  • 3.33 tablespoons sugar
  • 0.83 cup condensed milk
  • 3.33 tablespoons cheddar cheese grated
  • 3.33 cups coconut milk

Preparation

  • Step 1 Add the 2 cups coconut milk in the mixing bowl where the mixed ingredients are. Mix again.
  • Step 2 Grease the baking tray then pour-in the batter (these are the ingredients that you just mixed together).
  • Step 3 Pre -heat oven for 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes then put-in the baking tray with batter and bake for 1 hour. Remove from the oven and set aside.
  • Step 4 Meanwhile prepare the topping by combining 2 tablespoons sugar and flour in the heated saucepan.
  • Step 5 Pour-in 1/2 cup condensed milk then mix thoroughly.
  • Step 6 Add 2 tablespoons cheddar cheese while stirring constantly.
  • Step 7 Pour 2 cups of coconut milk and stir constantly for 10 minutes.
  • Step 8 Pour the topping over the Cassava Cake (baked batter) and spread evenly.
  • Step 9 Separate the yolk from the egg white of the remaining egg (weâ€™ll be needing the egg white only).
  • Step 10 Glaze the topping with the egg white using a basting brush. Simply dip the brush to the egg white and brush it on the cassava cake.
  • Step 11 Set your oven to broil mode. Broil the Cassava cake until color turns light brown.
  • Step 12 Garnish with extra grated cheese on top. Serve. Share and enjoy!
