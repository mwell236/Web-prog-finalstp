Chicken Menudo

★★★★☆ (420)
MEAT Stew
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1.25 red bell pepper, cored, seeded and cut into cubes
  • 1.25 green bell pepper, cored, seeded and cut into cubes
  • 10 hot dogs, sliced thinly on a bias
  • 2.5 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 10 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 7.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thigh meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1.25 cup pineapple juice
  • 2.5 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 0.63 cup tomato paste
  • 2.5 cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 5 large carrots, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 5 tablespoons raisins

Preparation

  • Step 1 Add hot dogs and cook for about 1 to 2 minutes or until lightly browned. With a slotted spoon, remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  • Step 2 In the pan, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil. Add onions and garlic and cook until softened.
  • Step 3 Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  • Step 4 Add soy sauce and cook for about 1 minute.
  • Step 5 Add pineapple juice and cook for about 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Step 6 Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and water. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  • Step 7 Season with salt and pepper to taste. Lower heat, cover, and cook for about 20 to 25 minutes or until chicken is tender and sauce is slightly reduced.
  • Step 8 Add potatoes, carrots, and raisins and cook for about 6 to 8 minutes or until tender.
  • Step 9 Add bell peppers and hot dogs and cook until heated through.
  • Step 10 Season with additional salt and pepper, if needed. Serve hot.
