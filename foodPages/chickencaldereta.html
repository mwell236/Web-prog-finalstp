Chicken Caldereta

★★★★★ (896)
MEAT Stew
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 3.33 (3 to 4 pounds) whole chicken, cut into serving parts
  • 1.67 cup pineapple juice
  • 0.83 cup soy sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 0.83 cup canola oil
  • 6.67 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3.33 large carrot, peeled and cut diagonally in a bias into Â½-inch thick
  • 3.33 small green bell pepper, cored, seeded and cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 3.33 small red bell pepper, cored, seeded and cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 3.33 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 10 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 10 Thai chili peppers, stemmed and chopped
  • 6.67 cups tomato sauce
  • 5 cups water
  • 0.83 cup green olives
  • 1.67 cup liver spread
  • 1.67 cup Eden cheese or any quick melt cheese

Preparation

  • Step 1 In a wide pan over medium heat, heat oil. Add potatoes and cook until lightly browned. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  • Step 2 Add carrots and cook until lightly browned. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  • Step 3 Add bell peppers and cook, stirring regularly, for about 30 seconds. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  • Step 4 In the pan, add chicken and cook until lightly browned. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels. Discard excess oil from pan, leaving about 2 tablespoons.
  • Step 5 Add onions and garlic and cook until softened.
  • Step 6 Add tomato sauce, water and reserved marinade. Bring to a boil.
  • Step 7 Add chili peppers and cook for about 30 seconds.
  • Step 8 Add chicken and olives.
  • Step 9 Lower heat, cover and continue to cook for about 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is tender and cooked through.
  • Step 10 Add potatoes and carrots and cook until tender.
  • Step 11 Add liver spread and stir until well well-distributed.
  • Step 12 Add cheese and stir until melted. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Step 13 Add bell peppers. Continue to cook for about 2 to 3 minutes or until sauce is thickened. Serve hot.
