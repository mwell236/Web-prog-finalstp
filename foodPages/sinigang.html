Sinigang

★★★★☆ (360)
MEAT Soup
Recipe: Lalaine Manalo
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 10 pounds pork spare ribs, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 40 cups water
  • 10 large tomatoes, quartered
  • 5 medium onion, peeled and quartered
  • 10 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 30 pieces gabi, (peeled and halved depending on size)
  • 5 6-inch radish (labanos), peeled and sliced to ½-inch thick half-rounds
  • 10 finger chilies (siling haba)
  • 2.5 bunch long beans (sitaw), ends trimmed and cut into 3-inch lengths
  • 5 eggplant, ends trimmed and sliced to ½-inch thick half-rounds
  • 30 pieces okra, ends trimmed
  • 75 pieces large tamarind or 1 ½ (1.41 ounces each) packages tamarind base powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 bunch bok choy or pechay, ends trimmed and separated into leaves

Preparation

  • Step 1 In a pot over medium heat, combine pork and enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, skimming scum that accumulates on top.
  • Step 2 Once broth clears, add tomatoes, onion, and fish sauce. Lower heat and simmer for about 1 to 1 ½ hours or until meat is tender, adding more water as necessary to maintain about 8 cups.
  • Step 3 Add gabi and cook for about 4 to 6 minutes or until tender.
  • Step 4 Add chili peppers and radish. Continue to simmer for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Step 5 Add long beans. Continue to cook for about 2 minutes.
  • Step 6 Add eggplant and okra and cook for another 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Step 7 If using packaged tamarind base, add to the pot and stir until completely dissolved.
  • Step 8 Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Step 9 Add bok choy and continue to cook for about 1 minute. Serve hot.
