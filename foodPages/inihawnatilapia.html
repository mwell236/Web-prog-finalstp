Inihaw na Tilapia

★★★★☆ (120)
SEAFOOD Grilled
Recipe: vanjo merano
Image: AI

Ingredients

  • 6 medium tilapia fish cleaned (scales, innards, and gills removed)
  • 3 to 4.5 teaspoons sea salt
  • A dash of ground black pepper
  • Cooking oil spray for grilling

Preparation

  • Step 1 Arrange the tilapia in a plate and rub salt and pepper all over the fish including its cavity.
  • Step 2 Spray cooking oil spray on the grill.
  • Step 3 Start to heat the grill.
  • Step 4 Grill each side of the tilapia for 10 minutes. Note: use a spatula to turn the fish over so that the sides will be intact and clean.
  • Step 5 Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with lime or lemon wedges.
  • Step 6 Share and enjoy!
